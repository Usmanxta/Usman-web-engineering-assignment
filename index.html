Empowering Education for Tomorrow's Leaders

PAF IAST is an initiative of the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in collaboration with Austrian and Chinese Universities. Our aim is to raise the quality of technical education in Pakistan through skill training and human resource development.

About PAF-IAST

A purpose-built campus with state-of-the-art facilities for interactive learning through smart classrooms and advanced laboratory equipment has been established in Haripur. PAF-IAST is at the fulcrum of the rapidly evolving global job market for highly skilled human resources, transforming Pakistan's youth into a national dividend.

At PAF IAST, we are committed to promoting equal opportunities and welcoming students from all backgrounds, irrespective of their creed, color, gender, ethnicity, and their social and financial status.

Scholarships

PAF IAST offers different types of local and foreign scholarship opportunities on a need and merit basis. Financial Aid is also granted to deserving candidates.

Internships

Mandatory for every student, to do an internship, every semester in an appropriate industry for practical experience related to their respective degree programs.
